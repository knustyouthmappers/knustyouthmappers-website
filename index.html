KNUST YouthMappers

KNUST YouthMappers is a chapter of the global YouthMappers network located in the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology in Kumasi, Ghana.

Donate Now

What we stand for

  • {% include icons/site.html icon="population" %}

    Community

    We strive to exchange and collaborate with other YouthMappers chapters and youth groups worldwide while offering ourselves as a resource and service to KNUST student organisations to add value through mapping. We aim to build a community which is diverse, passionate, and growing every day.

  • {% include icons/site.html icon="economy" %}

    Capacity Building

    The purpose of KNUST YouthMappers is to grant students the opportunity to improve skills in the field of mapping and create open geographic data and analyses that address locally defined development challenges as well as global ones.

  • {% include icons/site.html icon="conditions" %}

    Development

    Our vision is to cultivate a generation of young people to become leaders in creating resilient communities, and empowering them to define their world by mapping it.

Our Mission

The purpose of the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology YouthMappers is to use open source data and GIS technologies to map potential disaster areas, provide resources for emergency management, connect its members with open source and GIS organisations, and serve any upcoming needs of our project partners and the open source mapping community.
